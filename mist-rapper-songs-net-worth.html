    MIST is best known for being an award-winning rapper.

    Here is everything you need to know about the star…

    Who is Mist?

    Rhys Thomas Sylvester, born on March 2, 1992, is known professionally as Mist.

    He is a British rapper from Birmingham.

    Mist's father Steve Sylvester was a guitarist who played bass in The J.A.L.N alongside his brothers and appeared on Top Of The Pops.

    Their track Disco Music reached No21 in the UK charts in 1976.

    His mum died in November 2011, when Rhys was just 19.

    His father died of a cardiac arrest just three months later.

    Around this time he had his first child, a daughter.

    What are Mist's most popular songs?

    Mist’s second EP Diamond In The Dirt, released in 2018, peaked at number four in the UK Albums Chart.

    read more in music

    He collaborated with a number of notable names for the EP, including Jessie Ware, Mr Eazi, Not3s and Haile.

    Three songs from the EP entered the UK Singles Chart.

    His 2019 track So High featuring Fredo reached No7.

    What is Mist's net worth?

    It is thought that Mist's net worth is an estimated £2million.

    He earns his money through his music and touring.

    Is Mist on social media?

    Yes, Mist is on Instagram @mist_rs.

    You can find him on TikTok @mist__official.

    When did Mist get shot?

    At around 10pm on Tuesday, August 27, 2019, the MOBO award winner was shot in the leg during an alleged robbery thought to have been committed by two or three men inside the rented villa.

    Armed officers from two police forces, the GNR and PJ, forced their way into the villa to make sure the suspected burglars were not still inside.

    Two friends of the star are said to have driven him to Faro Hospital.

    The companions, a man and a woman, reported the incident to police at the hospital.
