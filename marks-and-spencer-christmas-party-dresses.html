    I'm 40, fat and fab - M&amp;S' green sparkly party dress is a winner &amp; everyone agrees they're 'killing

    A WOMAN who describes herself as fabulous, fat, and 40 plus has revealed her go-to outfits for the party season. And its Marks &amp; Spencer that comes out on top, thanks to their range of festive sequin outfits that would light up any party.

    12 Apr 2024, 00:00

    A WOMAN who describes herself as “fabulous, fat, and 40 plus ” has revealed her go-to outfits for the party season. 

    And it’s Marks & Spencer that comes out on top, thanks to their range of festive sequin outfits that would light up any party.

    Social media user @fabfatand40plus, real name Caroline Sigamoney, shares her “style tips and second hand finds” on her TikTok profile. 

    And now we’re counting down to Christmas parties evening out, she was keen to tell everyone what she would be wearing this December. 

    The TikToker shared a video on her account titled “Marks & Spencer Christmas party try on.”

    The woman, who wears a size 20, then added a string of hashtags, including #partyoutfitideas and #sequins.

    In the clip, @fabfatand40plus stepped out of M&S’ dressing rooms in nine different outfits.

    But it was one in particular that caught her followers’ eyes, as they hailed a green sequin dress she modelled as “stunning.” 

    The below-the-knee style dress - called the Sequin Round Neck Midaxi Column Dress is covered in sparkles, and costs £99.

    After seeing her strutting her stuff in the in-demand number, one person wrote: “Aww, the green dress!”

    Another person also complimented her on the look, as they typed: “Green dress is stunning on you.”

    And a third hailed it as their number one choice out of all of the other ensembles she showed.

    These included a pair of black faux leather cropped trousers (£39.50) with a silver sequin top (£29.50) and matching bomber jacket that's £79.

    She accessorised this with a sparkly black bag and simple silver pumps.

    Up next, she switched it up and went full out on the bottom with silver sequin slim fit flared trousers (£55).

    The TikTok user teamed this with a black silk blouse that featured a collared tie neck shirt (£39.50), and black clutch bag. 

    She then wore both parts of the suit together, adding the bomber to the sequin trousers.

    @fabfatand40plus then wore the leather trousers with a green jumper with sequin detail on both sleeves, which costs £35.

    Elsewhere, she wore a midi-style dress (Metallic Ruched Midi Bodycon Dress £35) that had smaller sequins all over in a gold colour, as well as a £49.50 one called the Sequin Ruched Midi Column Dress.

    As people rushed to like the video, everyone was in agreement that the supermarket are outdoing themselves in the fashion department this festive season. 

    One of her followers wrote: “M&S is killing it at the moment, especially party wear.”
