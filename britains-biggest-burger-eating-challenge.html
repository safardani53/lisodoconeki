    MEET the UK’s biggest burger — a 20lb whopper containing 10,000 calories.

    But foodies have found the Dino Burger a bite too much so far — with no one yet finishing the meal.

    The fattening feast is made with ten 500g packs of minced beef mixed with 400g of breadcrumbs.

    It comes covered with cheese, tomatoes and ­lettuce inside a huge, specially made bun — plus chips on the side.

    The artery-blocking onslaught costs £30 at Shepherd Place Farm in Doncaster —  but is free if diners can finish it all in one sitting.

    Its creator Ted Phillips said: “I’d wanted to do something like this for a while — it’s absolutely huge, even carrying it is a task.

    “It’s by far the biggest burger in the country.

    "No one has finished it yet. I don’t think anyone could even eat that much.”

    Among those to attempt the burger — and “fail miserably” — is food challenge YouTuber Kyle Gibson, known as Kyle V Food.

    Ted also created the UK’s biggest full English — the 17,000-calorie, 150-item Terminator Armageddon.
