    THEY were on countless Christmas lists this year.

    But for those not lucky enough to get a real Stanley cup, there's a new dupe in Tesco that's got shoppers racing to the supermarket.

    Holly took to TikTok to share a video showing the cups, which came in teal and blue in her Tesco's.

    And while they are priced up at £18, that's reduced to just £13.50 if you have a Clubcard - which is free to sign up for.

    "Girls, run don't walk to Tesco - £13.50 on Clubcard!" she wrote over the top of the clip.

    The cups are exactly the same shape and size as the Stanley, but have the word "Active" on the front.
