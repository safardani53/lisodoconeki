    As you probably know by now, Kylie Jenner and Blac Chyna aren’t exactly BFFs.

    In fact, the feud between these two has gotten so bad that their families are all up in it, and we’re not just talking about the fact that Rob Kardashian is banging Blac.

    Last week, Blac’s mom, who goes by the name of Toni Tokyo (sigh), got in on the action when she hinted that Tyga and Kylie are married in a bizarre, NSFW rant.

    Today, she decided to engage in some serious Kardashian-bashing going after not only Kylie but her entire family in a tirade addressed to Tyga:

    "Do you think your baby will be coming out looking like the playdough mold face they made for them hoes…DEAD WRONG!" Toni began. "A little surgery to enhance ok…FULL FACE CHANGES…TRY AGAIN!"

    So we guess it’s safe to say that the rumors about Blac being pregnant with Rob’s baby aren’t true. Otherwise, (we hope) Ms. Tokyo probably wouldn’t be making fun of the family’s genetics.

    Anyway, Toni didn’t stop there, of course. She went on for another several words or so, putting a serious strain on her caps lock key in the process:

    "@KimKardashian you weak ass b-tch! I am not a Hollywood hoe!! No handshakes or forgiveness EVER IN THIS LIFE!!! IF I EVER SEE HER SHE BETTER HAVE SECURITY!!!"

    You get the idea.

    Fortunately, Kim does travel with security, specifically so she’s safe from attacks from violent psychopaths like Toni Tokyo.

    Frankly, we think Toni should start keeping her tirades to herself. The way things are going, she might soon have a Kardashian son-in-law!

    Now that would make for some awkward Thanksgivings.
