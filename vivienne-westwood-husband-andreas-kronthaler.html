    FAMED fashion designer Vivienne Westwood helped bring modern punk and new-wave fashions into the mainstream.

    Tragically, she died on December 29, 2022, at the age of 81, leaving behind her beloved husband, Andreas Kronthaler, and now fans want to know all about him.

    Who is Vivienne Westwood's husband Andreas Kronthaler?

    After Vivienne divorced Derek Westwood in 1965, many thought she would never marry again.

    She had a long-running relationship with Malcolm McLaren but eventually found love with Andreas Kronthaler.

    They met while she was teaching at the Vienna School of Applied Art in 1988.

    Like Westwood, Kronthaler is a designer, and hails from Tyrol, Austria.

    He explained during an interview with the Evening Standard: "Don't expect anything from your other side, from your partner, your wife or husband. Because then you're both free to be...

    "...She never cared if I got pissed or went out.

    What was Vivienne Westwood's cause of death?

    Westwood has been in the spotlight for decades and quickly rose to fame after she

    made clothes for Malcolm McLaren's boutique on King's Road, which became famous as "SEX".

    She died on December 29, 2022, surrounded by her family.

    Her brand said in a social media statement: "Vivienne Westwood died today, peacefully and surrounded by her family, in Clapham, South London.

    "Vivienne continued to do the things she loved, up until the last moment, designing, working on her art, writing her book, and changing the world for the better.

    "She led an amazing life. Her innovation and impact over the last 6 years has been immense and will continue into the future."

    Kronthaler later released his own statement, saying: "I will continue with Vivienne in my heart.

    "We have been working until the end and she has given me plenty of things to get on with. Thank you darling."

    Despite Westwood's death being confirmed, the exact cause was not disclosed.
