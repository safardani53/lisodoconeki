    Mum makes incredible heart-shaped Christmas wreath, spending just 11 at Poundland and Home Bargain

    A MUM has revealed how she made an incredible heart-shaped wreath for just 11 by shopping around for bargains. One woman decided to change up her Christmas decoration this year, and shunned the traditional round wreath for a heart shaped one.

    01 Jun 2024, 00:00

    A MUM has revealed how she made an incredible heart-shaped wreath for just £11 by shopping around for bargains.

    One woman decided to change up her Christmas decoration this year, and shunned the traditional round wreath for a heart shaped one.

    She shopped around for materials and bought a plain wreath from Home Bargains for just £6.

    And the thrifty mum hit up Poundland for all her decorations, splashing £5 on fake snow, berries, pine cones and orange slices.

    She used a glue gun – which she also sourced from the budget store – and stuck everything down.

    Her creative skills have been praised online after she shared a snap of her wreath to Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains, where it’s racked up thousands of likes.

    She said: “Plain heart wreath from home bargains £6.

    “£5 of things to stick on from Poundland

    “All stuck down with a glue gun (also from Poundland). Total cost £11.”

    Hundreds of people commented on her post, with one person saying: “As a Florist that is beautiful you have a talent.

    Another wrote: “Wow well done that’s lovely

    A third said: “That’s gorgeous. Much nicer than any I’ve seen in the shops.”

    Another thought: “Nicest one l have seen well done.”

    And somebody else said: “Wow, beautiful and a real bargain, looks really expensive! You've inspired me to try myself.”

    After receiving amazing feedback, she confirmed she’d be handing some out to her friends and family.

    She added: “Wow thank you for all your lovely comments, I don’t know about selling them as I’m still very amateur to it, but I’ll be doing some for family and friends definitely."
