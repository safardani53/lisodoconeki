    A SKIMS shopper was shocked to find out just how tiny the brand's micro bikini actually is.

    TikTok user Sarita showed her followers the viral item, using tortilla chips "for scale."

    Sarita purchased the $28 Fits Everybody micro triangle bralette and the matching $14 micro thong.

    "So if you’ve been eyeing the Skims micro bikini and wondering about the size, these are tortilla chips for scale," the influencer said.

    She placed the Trader Joe's chips on each cup of the bikini as well as on the bottoms.

    In a follow-up clip, Sarita tried on the skimpy silver swimwear over her clothes.

    "I can’t really bring this up any higher. Normally, I would wear it probably up [higher]. This is the most comfortable at this level because if you look, this is all string," she explained.

    Sarita concluded: "It’s not going to work for anyone."

    While the TikToker said she would avoid wearing the look to the beach, she admitted she would probably utilize the top at a "private pool."

    She added: "These bottoms I would wear in my backyard for cute tan lines. I would never wear this in public."

    Most read in Fabulous

    TikTok users took to the comments section to share their thoughts on the viral bikini.

    "I am a DDD cup. If I wore that in public someone would call the police," joked one viewer.

    Another follower said: "My sister ordered this by accident and calls it the pirate eye patch lol."

    "Honestly, feel like this is great for tanning in your backyard. Otherwise, I’d feel sooooo uncomfortable in public," commented a third person.
