    KOURTNEY Kardashian has given fans a glimpse of her multi-million dollar California mansion.

    Kourtney, 44, took some snapshots of her patio and pool of her $9million home in Los Angeles.

    Posted to the KUWTK alum's Instagram story, she shared with her millions of followers what her view looks like every morning.

    In the first photo, Kourtney showed off her gorgeous patio featuring tons of furniture and plants.

    "There's no place like home," she captioned the image.

    In the following pic, the mother-of-three showed off more of her patio on a bright and sunny California day.

    SERENE SCENE

    Earlier this month, Kourt showed off her luxe living room with a gorgeous view of the backyard on social media.

    CHEFS KISS

    A few weeks ago, Kourtney captured pics of her giant kitchen inside her LA mansion that featured one of her children enjoying breakfast.

    The video, which was timestamped at 10:17 am, began inside the kitchen as Kourt showed off the pancakes frying on her gas-top stove.

    Astrud Gilberto's rendition of Fly Me To The Moon played in the background as she panned over her morning pastry, fresh pancake batter, and her stainless steel appliances to focus on the window.

    The following video showed her youngest son Reign, eight, eating his breakfast as The Kardashians star continued to show off the gorgeous exterior of her house.

    Toward the end of the recording, Kourtney pushed in toward a mug of peppermint tea that had the encouraging message "You are unlimited" printed on its tag.
