    Kyrie Andrew Irving is an American professional basketball player for the National Basketball Association's Brooklyn Nets. A seven-time All-Star member and two-time All-NBA member, he won the NBA Championship with the Cavaliers in 2016. Irving played college basketball for Duke Blue Devils before joining the Cavaliers in 5. 2011 . He won the first Valuable Player Award for All-Star Game 2014. In the 2016 NBA Finals, Irving set a championship record of 3 field points to complete a history of the Cavaliers returning to the Golden State Warriors. After another final in 2017, Irving requested a trade and was transferred to the Boston Celtics. He played as Celtic for two seasons, after which he signed for Brooklyn Nets as a free agent in 2019 .
