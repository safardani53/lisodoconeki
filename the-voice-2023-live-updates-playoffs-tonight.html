    TOM Nitti has spoken out after his unexpected exit from The Voice this week.

    In an emotional Instagram post, the former contestant opened up about his exit.

    "First and foremost I’m blessed beyond belief and I can’t say enough how much I love and appreciate the constant support from my fans, my friends and my family…" he wrote.

    "As much as it absolutely stings to say, I had to make probably one of the hardest yet easiest decisions ever and ultimately at the end of the day, my kids will always come first and if I had to make the same decision again I’d do it over and over."

    He went on to thank those who had his back through tough times, without getting into details.

    His coach on the show was Reba McEntire, who announced his departure on Monday.

    The Voice live shows begin next Monday at 8 pm ET on NBC. Fans can catch new episodes on Peacock, Fubo, Sling TV, and Hulu Live TV.

    • Full list of winners – Gina Miles

      In Niall Horan's first victory as a coach, Gina Miles won Season 23 of The Voice.

      Originally from Paxton, Illinois, the singer relocated to Sacramento shortly before auditioning for the program.

      Regarding her inspiration to try out for The Voice, she often credits her father’s DJing career as her primary influence.

    • Full list of winners – Bryce Leatherwood

      Bryce Leatherwood was crowned the champion of Season 22 on The Voice as a member of Team Blake.

      Leatherwood is a Woodstock native who grew up singing and playing the ukelele in middle school.

      Since winning the competition, Leatherwood has traveled around multiple Ole Red locations to perform country music.

      He signed with Republic Records and is now focused on moving to Nashville where he plans to continue his career in music.

    • Full list of winners – Girl Named Tom

      Girl Named Tom, is comprised of three siblings, Caleb, Joshua, and Bekah Liechty.

      The group is originally from South Bend, Indiana, and created their band’s name as a reference to Caleb and Joshua’s desire to have a little brother.

      Instead, Bekah was born, and as children, the boys called her Thomas while she was a baby.

      Girl Named Tom was on Kelly Clarkson’s team when they won Season 21 of The Voice.

    • Full list of winners – Cam Anthony

      After going viral on YouTube as a preteen, Cam Anthony won season 20 of The Voice as a member of Blake Shelton's team in 2021.

      The singer was born and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and was an internet sensation by the time he was 11.

    • Full list of winners – Carter Rubin

      The first winner from Gwen Stefani's team was 15-year-old Carter Rubin.

      He became the second youngest winner of The Voice ever, and the youngest male.

    • Full list of winners – Todd Tilghman

      Another Team Blake winner, Todd Tilghman was the oldest contestant to win The Voice at the age of 42 in 2020.

      The worship leader garnered the most votes during the final episode - which was filmed remotely amid the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.

    • Full list of winners – Jake Hoot

      Country singer Jake Hoot was a member of Kelly Clarkson‘s team when he won Season 17 of The Voice at 31 years old.

      A Texas native, Hoot relocated to live in Tennessee.

    • Full list of winners – Maelyn Jarmon

      American folk singer Maelyn Jarmon won Season 16 of The Voice at the age of 26 as a member of John Legend's team.

      Chevel Shepherd won Season 15 of The Voice when she was 19 years old as a member of Team Kelly.

    • Full list of winners – Brynn Cartelli

      Chris Blue is a soul singer who won Season 12 of The Voice.

      The singer won The Voice at the age of 27 and was a member of Alicia Keys‘ team.

      The rock-and-roll singer/songwriter auditioned with The Chain by Fleetwood Mac.

    • Full list of winners – Sundance Head

      Jason “Sundance” Head won Season 11 of The Voice as a member of Team Blake.

      The son of iconic singer Roy Head, Jason is no stranger to the music industry.

      He’s a country soul singer/songwriter from Texas.

      Alisan Porter brought home the first win for Christina Aguilera's team during Season 10.

      Before joining The Voice, the musician was a child actor - making appearances in Parenthood, Stella and I Love You to Death.

      Contemporary Christian singer Jordan Smith won Season 9 as a member of Adam Levine's team.

    • Full list of winners – Sawyer Fredericks

      Sawyer Fredericks won Season 8 of The Voice as a member of Pharrell Williams' team.

      The blues singer became the youngest male winner in the show's history at age 16.

    • Full list of winners – Craig Wayne Boyd

      Craig Wayne Boyd is a country singer who won Season 7 of The Voice as a part of Blake Shelton's team.

      Just two days after winning the show in 2014, the single dad met the woman who would become his wife.

      The couple now shares five children and the family lives in Nashville.

    • Full list of winners – Josh Kaufman

      The first Team Usher winner was Josh Kaufman, a soul singer from Florida, who won Season 6 of The Voice.

      Before joining Usher's team, the artist started out as a member of Team Adam.

    • Full list of winners – Tessanne Chin

      Tessanne Chin is a Jamaican-born singer who won Season 5 of The Voice as a member of Adam Levine's team.

      Danielle Bradbery brought home Team Blake's third win on Season 4 of The Voice in 2013.

      Blake Shelton's team got his second win in a row with country pop singer Cassadee Pope.
