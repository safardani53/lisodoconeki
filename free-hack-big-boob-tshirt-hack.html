    FRUMPY T-shirts are like security blankets, they're comfy and necessary, but you can't always rely on them for support.

    One woman explained that for women with big boobs, these shirts don't support or compliment their chest. Luckily, she shared a free hack that turns a baggy T-shirt into a flattering one.

    Plus-size influencer Latasha Lee connects her style expertise with body positivity.

    She encourages her followers to discover what clothes they feel good in and how to dress according to their body shape.

    Latasha posted a TikTok revealing her clever hack to transform any T-shirt into a favorable one for any woman with a big bust.

    All you need is an old shoelace.

    Latasha ties two shoelaces together to make her string longer, but depending on your shirt length, you may not need to.

    She loops the shoelace through the bottom of her shirt and up through the head hole.

    Then, Latasha ties the two ends of the lace together.

    Tie the lace tight to keep it secure.

    The lace sinches the shirt right in the middle.

    "Y'all see how good my waist look," Latasha asks viewers.

    Latasha says to pull the shoelace through the shirt once more and then wrap it behind your back.

    Once you cross the shoelace behind your back, bring them around the front.

    Latasha finishes by tying the lace in a bow.
