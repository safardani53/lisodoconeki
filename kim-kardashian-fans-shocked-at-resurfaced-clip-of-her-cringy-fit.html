    Fit In Your Jeans By Friday, a workout video by Kim was shared on an Instagram page dedicated to the Kardashian family, with the caption, “I was today years old when I found out Kim had a workout DVD *laughing crying emoji*.”

    Kim Kardashian Debuted Her New Workout Routine.

    Despite the fact that the video is 13 years old, most fans were surprised to learn she had one. “This has to be the funniest c**p ever!!” said one user. The post has received over 5,000 likes and 80 comments.

    Another person stated, “From this video, I discovered that she has a workout DVD. lol *laughing crying emoji* I want it now.” Other commenters were taken aback not only by the video but also by the fact that she also composed music. “But a month ago, I found out she had a song!!!!” someone said.

    Kim Kardashian’s Other Objectives

    Most people are familiar with Kim from Keeping Up With the Kardashians, but what else has she done? Aside from this totally cringe workout video, Kim has worked as a stylist for singer Brandy, endorsed Carl’s Jr. food products, has several clothing and fragrance collections, released a song, and much more.

    Kim Kardashian Exercise

    Moving onto her workout routine, Kim revealed her workout routine to Women’s Health in April 2021. Treadmill sprints are a new addition to her workout routine. Every morning at 6 a.m., the 41-year-old works out with her trainer, Melissa Alcantara. Instead of cardio, she focuses on resistance and core training. Kim posts her workouts on social media and no longer keeps her fans in the dark about her efforts to tone her body.
