    How can I buy tickets?

    Zach Williams will head on the road for a tour this fall across the United States, joining fellow musician Ben Fuller.

    Tickets are available to purchase via Zach's official website starting Friday, May 13, 2022.

    Where is Zach Williams headed on tour?

    From September through November, Zach Williams will travel across North America.

    Who is Zach Williams?

    Born on March 5, 1981, Zach Williams grew up in Arkansas.

    He began singing in the band Zach Williams & The Reformation in 2007, the group remaining together through 2012.

    In 2012, Zach dedicated his life to following Christianity.

    He and Robby Rigsbee, a guitarist in The Reformation, formed the group known as Brothers of Grace.

    Read More On The Sun

    Zach went solo in 2016 after signing to the Provident Label Group.

    His debut solo album, Chain Breaker, dropped in December of 2016.
