    Join Del Boy and Rodney on stage in once-in-a-life chance to cameo in the Only Fools and Horses Musi

    01 Jan 0001, 00:00

    LOVELY jubbly! You could get the chance of a lifetime to appear on the West End stage  in the Only Fools And Horses musical – thanks to The Sun.

    The lucky winner of our exclusive competition will tread the boards of the Theatre Royal Haymarket in the remake of the legendary sitcom, alongside favourite characters Del Boy, Rodney and Grandad.

    We are also giving the winner ten tickets so their friends and family can attend too – and a backstage tour to meet the cast.

    The show was written by Jim Sullivan, the son of the late Only Fools creator John, and comic Paul Whitehouse, who has also played Grandad.

    Spice Girl Mel B did a one-off appearance as part of Sky show  A League Of Their Own.

    She said:  “Whoever gets up on that stage will have the best time ever and will never forget it.”

    Mel  appeared  alongside A League Of Their Own regulars Jamie Redknapp, Freddie Flintoff and  comic Tom Allen as a challenge for A League Of Their Own Road Trip: Dingle To Dover, which is available now.  

    She explained: “I’ve performed Chicago on Broadway but this was a whole new ball game."

    “We just decided to go for it and have a laugh. We had to learn a dance routine and all the words to the theme tune.

    "I don’t think any of us were step  or word-perfect but we made up for it with  enthusiasm.

    “When we went on stage it was a total shock to the audience,  which made it even more funny.

    “And they were such a great crowd,  all cheering and clapping. There was a totally fantastic atmosphere.”

    And her advice to our lucky  winner?  “Don’t forget to enjoy yourself and smile!”
