    BETTY WHITE, The Golden Girls actress, passed away aged 99, weeks before her 100th birthday.

    White's co-star and apparent crush, Robert Redford, paid a heartwarming tribute to the beloved icon.

    What did Robert Redford say about Betty White?

    For years, Betty White reportedly admired actor Robert Redford from afar.

    In a December 28, 2021 interview with People, she admitted her crush on Redford saying, "I've heard Ryan [Reynolds] can't get over his thing for me but Robert Redford is The One."

    In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, White stated she wasn't looking to actually meet the star, because, "If I ever met him, I'd faint."

    Redford first heard of White's admiration while on an interview in 2017 with ET, to which he responded "I can't imagine being loved by anybody better."

    After her death, Redford said in a statement, "Betty lived life devoted to her craft and her love of animals. She made us all laugh, including me."
