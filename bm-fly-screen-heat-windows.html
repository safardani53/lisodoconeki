    There are plenty of hacks to getting rid of ants from your garden, but experts have now revealed the one ingredient that will banish them from your house.

    Garden Guides recommend using lavender throughout your home to both prevent and tackle an ant infestation.

    The scent of lavender is a deterrent to insects and is so overpowering to ants that it even masks the ever-tempting scent of food.

    There are various forms of lavender available to use in your home.

    You can sprinkle dried lavender leaves in areas of your home where ants frequent such as in cupboards, corners of the kitchen, and along with doorways and windows.

    You can also use lavender oil or make your own to spray these areas regularly.

    Read More on The Sun

    Garden Guides recommends using one part lavender to 10 parts olive oil when making your own mixture.

    If you want to go one step further you can grow your own lavender plant and harvest the leaves.
