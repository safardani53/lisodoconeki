    A WOMAN has revealed that she went to one of the best Primark's ever, in Newcastle Upon Tyne. 

    Posting on TikTok under the username @rebbeebee18, the ‘Newcastle lass’ was left open-mouthed when she visited the huge Primark and spotted a new Greggs café on the second floor.

    The travel and food fan took a clip of the new café, to show her followers.

    Not only is there a huge selection of our Greggs favourites, including meat bakes and sweet treats, there’s also a sausage roll swing too.

    The social media user shared her video with the caption: “Pasty lovers of Newcastle, this is one for you!

    “Tasty by Greggs has just opened in Primark on Northumberland Street, Newcastle, so you can grab your favourite Greggs treats and have a sit down while doing your shopping.
