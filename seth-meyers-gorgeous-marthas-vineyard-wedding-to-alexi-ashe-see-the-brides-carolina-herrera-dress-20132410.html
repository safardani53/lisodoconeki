    Almost two months ago, Seth Meyers wed his beautiful bride Alexi Ashe in a beautiful ceremony on Massachusetts’ Martha’s Vineyard. Vogue magazine captured the couple’s big day in their November issue, which hit stands on Oct. 22. 

    Ashe wore a capped sleeve custom Carolina Herrera gown and her bridesmaids wore a variety of Carolina Herrera archival-print dresses with Ancient Greek sandals. 

    Meyers, 39, donned a Ralph Lauren Black Label suit as he clutched his bride’s hand, grinning on his way back down the aisle after the outdoor ceremony.

    After the reception dinner, the 150 guests moved into the nearby barn for dancing and a surprise performance by country crooner Brad Paisley.
