    LAST-MINUTE Christmas shoppers, listen up.

    Asda has started a pre-Christmas sale and it's full of kid's toys for less than £10.

    Elysia, took to social media to alert parents of the mega sale so that you can stock up on Christmas presents at a fraction of the usual price.

    "Christmas Gift Ideas £10 or Under for Kids at Asda Part 1. Make sure you're following me for even more ideas," she said.

    She first found a gift set of white chocolate selection box including KitKats, Milkybars and Lion bars- all a firm favourite with the whole family currently on sale for just £2.50.

    There was also an adorable Encanto diary gift set for £10 as well as a mini Barbie backpack full of surprise goodies for £5.

    If your child was obsessed with the Barbie film this year - you'll be pleased to know there's also a Barbie Crystal ball gown science kit to keep them busy Christmas morning slashed to £6.

    Squishmallow lovers will also be thrilled to see that some of the plushies have gone down to £7 each - including an adorable corn on the cob teddy.

    For the boys - a Dynoforce action figurine with a car has been marked down to £7 as well as a Dough activity set for £6.

    Also up for grabs is an air-dry clay set for £7, and a make-your-own bouncy balls set for just £1.50 - perfect for a stocking filler.

    Elysia shared the budget-friendly buys on her TikTok account @elysia.official and parents were thrilled by the finds.

    One wrote: "Ohh bargains."

    "Fab ideas.," another commented.

    But it's not the only place already having a sale before Christmas.

    If you have a Tesco closer to you it might be worth heading there as they have also slashed prices on tonnes of toys.

    Samantha Mary showed off the up to 80% off sale in stores.

    Panning the camera around to show Monopoly on a shelf, she continued: “This Monopoly set here, look, was £30 and is reduced to £4.99!”

    She then moved on to show a Trolls World Tour tiny dancers set, which was down from £17 to just £4.25. 

    The RainBoCorn surprise egg was 40 percent off, bringing the price down from £25 to just £15. 

    She then turned her attention to an Alice Doll that comes complete with a ‘magical oven’. 

    “That one there, 60 percent off,” she said before showing the price, which had come down from £49.99 to £19.99. 

    “This Barbie set here - again, 60 percent off,” she went on.
