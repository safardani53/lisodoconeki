    Adam Devine’s love for Chloe Bridges is no laughing matter. He and the ‘Pretty Little Liars’ alum have found love in Hollywood, so check out pictures of this sweet couple.

    Ever since they first met in 2014, Adam Devine and Chloe Bridges have been the perfect example of Hollywood life. The two met on the set of the film ‘The Final Girls,’ and began dating shortly afterwards. Over the next seven years, the two appeared on red carpets together, at events, and on each other’s Instagram pages. In 2019, Adam decided that Chloe was the one for him, and he got down on one knee. ‘She said yes!’ he shared with his followers in October 2019, along with that she actually said “ ‘Ahh Adam!’” before kissing him. Adam called Chloe his ‘best friend’ and he was ‘thrilled you want to get old and wrinkly with me!’ Unfortunately, the COVID-19 pandemic forced Adam and Chloe to postpone their nuptials until 2021. In October 2021, nearly two years after Adam proposed, the couple tied the knot. Photos of the wedding ceremony were shared by Adam’s ‘Workaholics’ costars, along with messages of love.
