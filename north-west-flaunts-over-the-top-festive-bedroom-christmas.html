    NORTH West has flaunted her over-the-top festive bedroom inside her mom Kim Kardashian's $60million mansion.

    The preteen gave fans a glimpse of the bedroom, including its nine Christmas trees and the piles of gifts collected around the area, with a TikTok on Thursday.

    The holiday video, which featured a sped-up version of Ariana Grande's Santa Tell Me, was posted to Kim, 43, and North's shared TikTok account.

    During its approximately 30-second runtime, the camera panned around the room, appropriately titled "North Pole" and captured all of its "Holiday decor."

    A pink-themed Christmas tree was shown in the opening moments of the recording.

    The ornately decorated tree featured all pink embellishments, including pink ornaments, pink bulbs, and pink ribbon.

    Several Christmas presents, wrapped in all-white gift paper, were already placed underneath the tree.

    Beside the pink tree was a smaller white, silver, and gold-decorated spruce with gifts covered with silver wrapping paper stuffed beneath it.

    The desk between the trees was covered with fake snow, Santa cookies, and tabletop ornaments.

    Two large Christmas pines were raised on both sides of North's bed.

    They were decorated with traditional holiday colors.

    On the bed, Santa and elf-shaped pillows were placed near the head while a plaid blanket was laid overtop.

    Another pair of Christmas trees were positioned on the other side of the children's room.

    One showcased an all-white color palette while the other was again decorated in a pastel pink coloration.

    More gifts were placed underneath each tree.

    Before the video was over, the camera also captured a trio of Christmas stockings meticulously hung and spaced beneath a wall-mounted television.

    Kim and Kanye divorced in 2022. The ex-couple share four children together: North, Chicago, Saint, and Psalm.
