    Watch The Trailer For Dora The Explorer's All-New Theatrical Short Film

    Dora and the Fantastical Creatures premieres Friday, Sept. 29, exclusively in theaters with PAW Patrol: The Mighty Movie, heralding the heroine's spring 2024 return in all-new Dora series on Paramount+.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Dora and the Fantastical Creatures premieres Friday, Sept. 29, exclusively in theaters with PAW Patrol: The Mighty Movie, heralding the heroine's spring 2024 return in all-new Dora series on Paramount+.

    Grab your backpacks, because Dora is gearing up for new adventures! 


    Nickelodeon's beloved Latina heroine, Dora, is making her big screen debut with the launch of the all-new animated short film, Dora and the Fantastical Creatures, exclusively in theaters beginning Friday, Sept. 29. 

    Movie fans of all ages around the world seeing PAW Patrol: The Mighty Movie, will experience the original theatrical short that champions her return in Dora, an all-new CG-animated preschool series set to stream exclusively on Paramount+ in spring 2024 in the U.S. 

    STREAM: Dora The Explorer On Paramount+
     
    In Dora and the Fantastical Creatures, Dora (Diana Zermeño) and Boots (Asher Spence) embark on an incredible adventure to the land of alebrijes, the most magical and colorful creatures in the rainforest. There, they must band together against Swiper (Marc Weiner) to save the beloved alebrijes and their Copal Tree Celebration. The short film also features: Anairis Quiñones as Luna Arcoiris; Danny Burstein as Sol; and Kate del Castillo as Ale. 
       
    Also joining the cast of Dora and the Fantastical Creatures and the new Dora series are additional voice actors, including: Anairis Quiñones as Map and The Fiesta Trio's armadillo; Maria Canals-Barrera as Abuela; Danny Burstein as Grumpy Old Troll, and The Fiesta Trio's frog and marmoset; Katarina Sky as Backpack; Donovan Monzon-Sanders as Tico; Tandi Fomukong as Isa; Quintún Muñoz as Benny; and Chris Gifford as Big Red Chicken.   

    Dora and the Fantastical Creatures is produced by Nickelodeon Animation in Burbank, Calif. William Mata is directing from a screenplay by Alejandro Bien-Willner and a story by Bien-Willner and Sean Gill. Production is overseen by Eryk Casemiro, Executive Vice President, Global Animated Series, Nickelodeon. Miriam Ritchie and Marielle Kaar serve as Nickelodeon's Executives in Charge of Production for the short. Dora the Explorer is created by Chris Gifford, Valerie Walsh Valdes and Eric Weiner.

    SUBSCRIBE: Sign Up For Paramount+

    Get ready to jump into a brand new world of Dora, starting with the theatrical premiere of Dora and the Fantastical Creatures on Friday, Sept. 29, only in theatres with PAW Patrol: The Mighty Movie! 

    In the meantime, you can adventure with Dora the Explorer at home! Stream episodes of the original series anytime on Paramount+.
