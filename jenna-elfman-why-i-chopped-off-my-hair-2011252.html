    Jennifer Aniston isn't the only one taking a big hair plunge. Jenna Elfman recently snipped her shoulder-length mane into a sexy pixie cut.

    "I’ve just been fantasizing about it since I was eleven –about having hair this short," the Friends With Benefits actress, 39, told UsMagazine.com of her new 'do at The Hollywood Reporter's Nominees' Night party Thursday. "And I honestly just never had the courage until lately."

    And now that she's made the big snip — inspired by fashion model Britt Maren — Elfman is loving it.

    "It felt liberating because there is also nothing you can do about it," she said.
