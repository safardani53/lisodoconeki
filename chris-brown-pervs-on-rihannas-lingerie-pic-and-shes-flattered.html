    It’s been a while since we last checked in on Chris Brown, but if we were beginning to doubt that he’s still the worst, Breezy’s latest Instagram activity would be sufficient to convince us that he remains one of the douchiest human beings on the planet.

    We probably don’t need to remind you that Brown brutally beat Rihanna while the two of them were dating back in 2009.

    It’s one of those stories that’s so horrible you hate to even dredge it up again and reflect on it.

    But to fully appreciate what a complete asshat Brown is in this scenario, it’s important to remember that Rihanna will likely bear the psychological scars of the attack for the rest of her life.

    On a lighter note, RiRi has been posting lots of lingerie photos on her Instagram page lately, and for that, we thank her.

    The racy pics are part of the promotional campaign for the singer’s Fenty lingerie line.

    Of course, we’re sure many fans who are not currently in the market for skimpy underthings are still finding a lot to love about the pics.

    One of those people is Chris Brown, who went absolutely bonkers over the pic above in a series of comments.

    “TO BREAK DA INTERNET! KEEP RISING QUEEN,” Chris wrote.

    “MUSIC SOON PLEASE," Brown later added.

    Not surprisingly, Chris took a lot of flak for publicly gushing about his abuse victim.

    “@chrisbrownofficial get a job!!! Stay away from her !!” commented one user.

    “Actually the audacity coming from you," another remarked.

    Obviously, that’s the proper reaction to all this mess.

    But amazingly, it seems RiRi was not offended by her ex’s compliments.

    “Rihanna is flattered by Chris’ comments on her Instagram,” one insider tells Hollywood Life.

    “She is well aware when he views her stories. Despite their rocky past, she keeps an eye on him keeping an eye on her," the insider adds.

    To make matters worse, it seems that Rihanna and Chris have maintained ties, and she continues to value his opinion:

    "It makes her feel good to know that he still thinks about her, cause she thinks about him too," says the source.

    "She has never blocked him from her social media, she likes that he still cares.”

    And with that, all of our "Rihanna in lingerie dreams" turned into "Chris Brown is still getting away with sooooo much" nightmares.
