    TEEN Mom Young and Pregnant star Kiaya Elliott mourned her father’s death on his 43rd birthday.

    The 21-year-old told fans “I miss my daddy so f**king much.”

    Kiaya took to her Instagram Story on Monday to reveal she is struggling with the passing of her father.

    Alongside a photo of a dark room, the MTV star wrote: “Dawg I miss my daddy so f**king much.”

    After adding three crying emojis, she concluded: “Lord help me.”

    Kiaya also wished her late father a happy birthday by sharing a series of photos.

    In the first photo, Kiaya’s father wrapped his arm around her as they posed for a family photo.

    'I MISS YOU'

    The Teen Mom star shared more throwback photos on her Instagram Story, including one of her father holding her two-year-old son Amour.

    After sharing that his birthday is on Monday, she wrote: “I miss you, like everyday.”

    Additionally, Kevo Muney’s song Leave Some Day played as Kiaya shared a throwback pair of the father-daughter duo.

    A following photo showed Kiaya’s dad posing with a friend, while the final throwback snap she shared captured her late father spending time with friends as 1st Lady’s Missing You played.

    Kiaya rose to fame while starring on MTV’s Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant.

    BABY DADDY DRAMA

    Back in July, she gave fans an update about her son’s relationship with his father- who is currently in prison.

    While participating in a Q&A session with fans on her Instagram Story, one person asked Kiaya if her son's father had been released from prison and if he's bonded with their son.

    She replied: "He is still very much incarcerated. Covid 19 is preventing visitation at most prisons but he'll be home 2022 or 2023"

    RadarOnline.com previously reported that the Teen Mom: Young & Pregnant star's baby daddy, X’Zayveon Gambrell, would not be released from prison until 2023.

    X'Zayveon, who was 17 at the time, was arrested in 2018 for possession of a firearm, aiding in/receiving a stolen firearm and possession of a weapon.

    His release date is set for January 9, 2023.

    X'Zayveon was arrested shortly after the TV star announced she was expecting their child.

    LEGAL TROUBLES

    Kiaya previously got into her own legal troubles after she was reportedly arrested on three assault and gun charges.

    She poked fun at her mugshot in a TikTok clip and explained that the arrest happened while she was in the middle of taking out her cornrows.

    The Ashley's Reality Round-Up reported the TV personality was arrested after a fight allegedly broke out at a party in Virginia on March 21.

    Kiaya reportedly got involved in the altercation while attending the bash with her on-off girlfriend Teazha.

    “A guy eventually jumped into the fight and Kiaya claimed he pulled out a gun and pointed it at her,” a source told the outlet.

    “Kiaya wasn’t really injured, but she did file charges against the guy for assault and battery and for pointing a gun at her.”
