    On NCIS Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 4, we finally learned who has been targeting the team’s matriarch, the diminutive but resourceful and resilient Hetty.

    Watch NCIS: Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 4 Online

    Titled "The 3rd Choir," NCIS Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 4 continued the pursuit of the persons or persons bent on taking down the team’s captain.

    At last, we learned that it is none other than Mattias Draeger (Jurgen Prochnow) who has marked her for elimination and caused so much destruction.

    The team members frantically searched for Mattias all over greater L.A. to find him, end his mission and bring an end to the mayhem once and for all.

    Hetty, as she always seems to do, got the last laugh.

    Proving once again that she’s never to be underestimated, she actually jumped into a dumpster in order to avoid capture, and that’s not even all:

     

    Lange peaced out of the Congressional hearings in which she had bogged down, and even had the honor of shooting Mattias in the leg in the end.

    Score another one for Hetty … end game, Mattias.

    If you watch NCIS: Los Angeles online, you know she’s not a woman to mess with, no matter how much or how many people you throw at her.

    As for Deeks and Kensi, they came in to work together, which Granger noticed, but the state of their romance or lack thereof is still a little bit ambiguous.

    At the same time, Chief Investigator Ava Wallace (Aisha Hinds) showed some interest on Sam, not aware that he is married, as his file does not say as much.

    Meanwhile, recurring player Dr. Nate Getz (Peter Cambor) paid a visit to Nell to help her get past the trauma that she recently experienced on a mission.

    If you recall, the operations expert went undercover as Hetty and almost died in the process, which is standard for G. or Sam but a total shock to Nell.

    Fortunately, Nate was there to lend his expertise, as the psychologist often does in his periodic returns to the team he once worked with full time.
