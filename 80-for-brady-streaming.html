    When will '80s for Brady' be streaming? Here's what we know about the hilarious comedy led by Jane Fonda, Rita Moreno, Lily Tomlin, and Sally Field.

    By Katherine Stinson

    Apr. 4 2023, Updated 11:31 a.m. ET

    Four women. One quarterback. A Super Bowl to remember. 80 for Brady is the girls' night out movie we didn't even know we needed.

    The story follows four dedicated Tom Brady fans (played by Rita Moreno, Sally Field, Jane Fonda, and Lily Tomlin), who decide to take life by the proverbial reins and head to what could be Tom Brady's last Super Bowl in 2017 (little did they know!).

    Article continues below advertisement

    If you're not a fan of sitting amongst strangers while eating overpriced snacks, here's what you need to know about watching the flick from the comfort of your own couch. And good news: the film is finally available for viewing at home after its theatrical run.

    Article continues below advertisement

    When and where will '80 for Brady' be on streaming?

    80 for Brady was distributed by Paramount Pictures and is now available to stream on the Paramount Plus platform. The film hit streaming services on April 4, 2023.

    You can also watch the movie on Amazon Prime or The Roku Channel if you sign up for a seven-day free trial of MGM.

    Article continues below advertisement

    What are fans and critics saying about '80 for Brady'?

    Is 80 for Brady worth watching? Fans seem to think so, based on the film's 89 percent audience rating on Rotten Tomatoes. The film has a 60 percent critical rating, which isn't great, but still not too shabby. Though the narrative may fall a little short, leaving audiences yearning for a little more complexity, the on-screen legends are strong enough to compensate for a lacking script. Their chemistry is palpable and their comedic delivery is on point.

    And, you know what? Any movie celebrating female friendship, football, and an unwavering zest for life is better than a Hail Mary pass at overtime.

    You can stream 80 for Brady on Paramount Plus now.
