    RAPPER Doja Cat gets the cream alongside ­Kendall Jenner as stars of fashion magazine Harper’s Bazaar’s Icons issue.

    Doja wore a black Versace dress for the mag while Kendall teamed a lilac polo neck with black tights and some sparkly knickers.

    The pair, both 27, were honoured along with Irish actor Paul Mescal, also 27.

    Calvin Klein model Kendall was chosen for her stellar career and business moves — including her best-selling tequila brand.

    She said of modelling: “It’s not always easy.”

    Kendall previously showed off her bare butt and fit figure for a sultry Calvin Klein ad.

    The reality star took to her Instagram page to share clips and photos from the underwear campaign with the intimates brand.

    The TV personality posed in a pair of basic gray Calvin panties, complete with the wide elastic strap and very little butt coverage.

    Kendall covered up in a classic white Calvin T-shirt with natural hair and light makeup for the shoot.

    She stood in front of a plain white wall background and flaunted her flat stomach in the look.
