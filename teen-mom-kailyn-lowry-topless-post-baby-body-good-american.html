    KAILYN Lowry flaunted her post-baby body in a topless Instagram post just two months after she gave birth to her fourth child. 

    After welcoming her newborn son, Creed, with ex Chris Lopez on July 30, the Teen Mom star revealed she “finally” fits back into her pre-pregnancy jeans. 

    Kailyn, 28, showed off her figure on Sunday in a skin-baring shot on her Instagram Stories. 

    In the picture, the mom-of-four showed off her stomach wearing only a grey bra as a top. 

    She put her cleavage on full display and showed off her two sleeves of tattoos in the mirror selfie. 

    The reality star wore denim-washed cutoff jeans from Khloe Kardashian’s clothing line, Good American. 

    One of the MTV personality’s followers asked how tall she is. 

    “5’8” & finally fitting back into my @goodamerican jeans,” the long-haired brunette responded alongside the bathroom picture. 

    Though she does not reveal how much weight she lost since giving birth to her baby boy Creed, just two weeks before his birth, she revealed she was the “heaviest” she had ever been.

    The outspoken TV star admitted during a July episode of her Coffee Convos podcast with Lindsie Chrisley that she’s at the "heaviest weight" of her life.

    She said: "I am at the heaviest weight I’ve ever been - before I got pregnant and then obviously now while I’m pregnant.

    "So that’s been a little hard, but also I haven’t had any real complications. Like, I just haven’t.

    “My glucose is good. All my stuff is good. So I went into the doctor today and I saw a new doctor.”

    During the appointment - which took place when she was 37 weeks pregnant - the doctor warned Kail about monitoring her blood levels ahead of giving birth.

    The Pennsylvania native shares Creed and son Lux, 3, with ex Chris Lopez.

    She is also the mother to 10-year-old son Isaac with ex Jo Rivera and 6-year-old son Lincoln with ex-husband Javi Marroquin.

    Kailyn recently called out her baby daddy Chris on social media for cutting their oldest son’s locks without asking her first.

    The furious mama bear shared a "before" shot of Lux's long hair followed by an "after" photo of it with a few inches missing.

    "Parenting with a narcissist be like…," the MTV star captioned the second picture.
