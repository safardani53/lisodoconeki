    Season 1 Episode 1 The Butcher of Blavikin

    Geralt of Rivia enters the city of Blaviken with the head of a kikimora - a creature that lives in swamps and underground. 

    Hoping to exchange his deceased trophy for some money, he meets Stregobor in his mansion. Setting the show's nudity level high from episode one, we see Stregbor in his mansion in the middle of a magical sexual illusion. Surrounded by supple naked young women who are picking apples from the garden of Eden.

    Paying no interest to the prized carcass, Stregobor wants to share a story about The Curse of the Black Sun - a curse that primarily affects princesses and can sway them to the dark side.

    He shares this with The Witcher as he expects him to look for a princess named Renfri, who is under the curse's influence and is out looking for him.

    Geralt doesn't want to concern himself with this and disagrees with killing the princess. However, later in the episode, Geralt runs into Renfri in the woods, where she shares her side of the story.

    The Witcher claims that there is no lesser evil and manages to convince her to leave the town of Kovir. 

    Coming around to his way of thinking, their tension increases and the pair end up having sex out in the woods. 

    More nudity is seen in the brief illusion than during their escapade in the woods.

    After their physical engagement, Geralt gets visions of Renfri telling him about "a girl in the woods who is his destiny." Renfri leaves in the morning and with the events that follow, Geralt acquires the title of The Butcher of Blaviken.

    Episode two focuses more on character development than that of sexual voyages. In particular we learn more about Yennefer, a witch who pops up more and more as the series goes on.
