    NETFLIX fans have been left feeling sick over horror movie Tusk, in which Justin Long is kidnapped and tortured.

    The Dodgeball actor stars in the 2014 film that has left a whole new audience terrified after landing on Netflix last month.

    Justin plays cocky podcaster Wallace who gets more than he bargained for when he travels to Canada to interview a mysterious recluse.

    Kill Bill star Michael Parks plays the creepy retired sailor and tells Wallace of the time when a walrus named “Mr Tusk” saved his life.

    Wallace drinks a tea laced with drugs and when he finally wakes up, his leg has been amputated and disturbed Howard reveals his plan to turn him into Mr Tusk, using the bones from his leg as his protruding tusks.

    The movie is billed as a comedy horror, but it's safe to say there were few people laughing when they reacted to it on Twitter.

    One said: "Watch Tusk that movie was so disturbing and creepy I almost threw up."

    Another said: "I've seen a LOT of weird movies but like.. Tusk? That's a WEIRD F*****G MOVIE."

    And a third said: "I need everyone to watch the movie tusk so that we can all be traumatized together. I have been thinking about that movie for almost a week."

    Horror fans have been making the most of Netflix of late, with films – both good and bad – keeping them glued to their sofa.

    And horror movie, No Escape Room, has left them sweating at home.

    They better make the most of it considering Netflix is having a massive cull of its available titles this year - both original and purchased films and TV shows.
