    Southern Charm season 9 promised drama and it’s safe to say this year’s show delivered. Andy Cohen is set to unpack all the juicy details at the two-part reunion airing on January 11. While fans see Olivia and Taylor hash out their beef with their former flames at the reunion, the two have now moved on in their love lives.

    Throwing it back to Southern Charm season 8, Olivia was a newbie dating Austen Kroll. The two didn’t stay together and split before the season 8 reunion show. Over a year later, the season 9 reunion is set to arrive, and Olivia, Austen, and Taylor are in the midst of relationship drama on the Bravo show.

    Who is Taylor from Southern Charm dating now?

    Much of the Southern Charm season 9 drama was centered around Taylor and Austen’s kiss after he broke up with Olivia.

    The two kissed in the fall of 2022, writes StyleCaster.

    Now, Taylor has a new boyfriend who has a loose link to the reality TV world.

    As of October 2023, she’s dating TJ Dinch’s roommate Gaston Rojas.

    In an exclusive interview with The Daily Dish, she explained: “I am seeing somebody. I am currently dating, so things are going really well…”

    View Instagram Post

    Olivia Flowers’ boyfriend

    Southern Charm’s Olivia went through a lot during season 9. The reality TV star mourned the loss of her brother and also navigated her ex-boyfriend and former friend’s kissing scandal.

    During the December 19 episode of Not Skinny But Not Fat with Amanda Hirsch, Olivia explained where she stands with Taylor Ann Green today.

    She said: “I didn’t want this for Taylor and I at all.”

    However, she added the two “gave up” on trying to repair their friendship: “We haven’t talked really since filming.”

    The Southern Charmer continued she and Taylor’s moms are good friends despite their fall out.

    Things have changed a lot for Olivia since season 9 wrapped filming in her personal and romantic relationships. The star shared a kiss with Rod Razavi in season 9. But, her love life took another turn since then.

    She’s now dating “someone not on reality TV,” and the two have been together for seven months. Olivia explained to Amanda her new boo “lives in Dallas,” and “works in finance remotely,” so he has the option to spend time with her in Charleston.

    Reality Titbit and GRV Media reached out to Taylor Ann Green for comment.

    View Instagram Post

    Fans estimate when Southern Charm reunion was filmed

    The relationship drama of season 9 was a lot to handle for viewers at home, let alone the cast themselves.

    Prior to the show, JT Thomas went through a painful divorce and he’s now professing his love for Taylor at the show’s finale, reports People.

    With all of the finale and reunion drama airing this January, fans are naturally curious to find out when the show’s two-part reunion was filmed.

    A Reddit thread created by fans sees some estimating that it was filmed in early December 2023.

    More commented: “It was filmed recently because the cast photos from the reunion came out like around Christmas.”
