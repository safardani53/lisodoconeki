    CHIPOTLE'S newest menu item introduces a new flavor that pairs incredibly with all your usual favorites.

    The restaurant is now offering garlic guajillo steak.

    Today Chipotle introduced the new option across the US, Canada, and even the world of the online game Roblox.

    The steak is finished with fresh lime and hand-chopped cilantro.

    For a burrito, bowl, or tacos, the Garlic Guajillo steak will cost $12.80, and for a quesadilla, it will be $13.35.

    I got to try this new option before it made its debut and it blew me away.

    Read more taste tests

    I ordered a bowl with rice, black beans, corn salsa, sour cream, and cheese.

    The slices of steak were really tender, and the guajillo peppers complimented the flavors of the signature cilantro rice and corn salsa.

    Although peppers are the main ingredient, this isn't spicier than the regular Chipotle steak option.

    It's similar to any Carne Asada option you'd find at a typical Mexican restaurant.

    Most read in Money

    If you've been to Moe's or Qdoba and had those steak options, you'll definitely enjoy this one.

    From September 17 through 25, Chipotle is offering free delivery on all garlic guajillo steak orders placed through its app or website.

    Chipotle Rewards members in the US and Canada might get the chance to try the guajillo steak for free.

    On September 14, Chipotle is dropping 25,000 codes for free meals at 7am and 3pm PT.

    The codes can be found and redeemed on the Chipotle app and websites.

    The chain hasn't yet confirmed how long the codes will be valid.

    Chipotle in the metaverse

    Chipotle is also debuting garlic guajillo steak in the metaverse through the Chipotle Grill Simulator experience on Roblox.  

    Roblox players can go to Chipotle's virtual kitchen where head chef Nevielle Panthaky will walk them through the cooking experience.

    Players will get to don their own virtual Chipotle uniform and can unlock new ones and exclusive designs as they make their way through the experience.

    Once they step up to the plancha, visitors will need to perfectly grill and season as many steaks as they can before time runs out.

    Then they'll try to beat the clock to cut as many steaks into strips as they can.

    In order to complete the task and fill up the progress bar, the virtual chefs will have to mix the steak with lime and cilantro.

    They'll get a virtual fork to taste their new creation, and a "flavor aura" will appear around their character.
