    Peeps brings back nostalgic holiday favorites just in time for Christmas and they're not all shaped

    PEEPS has announced the return of seasonal-inspired shapes and nostalgic flavors of their iconic marshmallows to retailers.

    The marshmallow candies will be available in a variety of flavors and several different stores.

    "Celebrate the holidays with the sweet shapes and festive flavors of Peeps Marshmallow," Peeps stated in an official statement.

    Peeps is iconically known for their marshmallow candies shaped like chicks and bunnies.

    This year, the company is participating in the Christmas spirit and released a variety of flavored marshmallows in different shapes.

    They would be perfect to top off your hot chocolates too.

    Christmas Peeps

    Peeps lovers can expect to see marshmallow snowmen, trees, gingerbread men, and stockings to appear on the shelves again.

    If you want to triple the chocolate flavors, you can add Peeps Delights Double Hot Chocolate Marshmallow Dipped in dark chocolate in your hot cocoa.

    Another holiday Peeps returning is the Delights Peppermint Bark Flavored Marshmallow dipped in dark chocolate.

    Where to buy them?

    You can almost get the holiday peeps at any grocery stores now.

    Target, Walmart, and Five Below all have the holiday peeps in stock.

    Target and Five below both sell marshmallow snowmen for $1.50.

    But if you want a bundle, you can buy the pack of four from Walmart for $12.88, which includes the Christmas tree, Gingerbread, Stockings, and snowmen-shaped marshmallows.

    More seasonal treats

    Besides candies, big chains launched their holiday treats too.

    Starbucks announced its holiday lineup earlier in November.

    Customer favorites like Peppermint Mocha and the Irish Cream Cold Brew returned to stores.

    Starbucks is introducing a new treat called Chocolate Pistachio Swirl made on a brioche bun.

    On the other hand, Dunkin' introduced a new Cookie Butter Cold Brew and Cookie Butter Donut to their holiday menu.
