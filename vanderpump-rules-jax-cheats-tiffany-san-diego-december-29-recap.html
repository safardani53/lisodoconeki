    ‘Vanderpump Rules’: Jax Cheats On Tiffany In San Diego — December 29 Recap

    Once a cheater, always a cheater? All signs are pointing to yes when it comes to Jax.

    It’s pretty odd to care for a girl we’ve only seen in one episode, but Jax should be ashamed of himself for cheating on Tiffany, who went out of her way to set Jax, Tom Sandoval, Tom Schwartz and Peter up in a fancy hotel in San Diego. Apparently, she works in the hospitality industry and gets amazing perks.

    But instead of appreciating her generosity, Jax did the opposite. However, he denies cheating on Tiffany. Instead, his version includes a girl throwing up in his hotel room’s bathroom, and her inappropriately trying to touch him.

    But even that version upset Tiffany. When she got wind of what happened, she immediately hung up on the serial cheater. Is Tiffany done with Jax for good? We’ll have to find out next week.

    Jax also managed to piss off Tom Sandoval because if it had been any one of them, he believes Jax would be throwing them under the bus and shouting about their indiscretions from rooftops.

    Scheana’s Wedding Causes Friction Amongst Her Co-Workers

    Meanwhile, after talking to Lisa Vanderpump, Scheana Marie decided to invite Katie Maloney to her bridal shower and bachelorette party. Initially, she had only invited Tom Schwartz to her wedding, but Lisa told her that’s rude and convinced her to change her mind. She did, however, inviting Stassi was a different story.

    Scheana met with Stassi for a drink and tried, once again, to patch things up. Unfortunately, but yet not surprisingly, Stassi said she has no interest in ever being friends with Scheana, whom she said looked like a “gypsy” during their meet-up.

    And as she walked out of the restaurant, Stassi said she won’t miss Scheana’s eyelashes, turbin, or leotards. (Ouch!)

    What do YOU think, HollywoodLifers? Should Stassi be friends with Scheana? Did Jax cheat on Tiffany? And if so, should she dump him?
