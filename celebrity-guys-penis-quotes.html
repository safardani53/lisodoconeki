    Paul Rudd

    During a video chat with Avengers: Endgame costar Chris Evans for Variety's Actors on Actors issue, the Ant-Man star kept quiet when it came to plans for a third installment of the Marvel franchise — but jokingly did spill some other details.

    After Rudd admitted he's "not going to be able to say anything" about another Ant-Man movie, Evans pivoted to another question, according to E!

    "I might as well ask you what your paychecks are," Evans said, before joking, "I don't know. Paul, what's your penis size?"

    "It's even bigger than my paycheck," Rudd quipped in response. "Put in your own Ant-Man joke there."

    Pete Davidson

    The Saturday Night Live star's former fiancée Ariana Grande stirred up quite a bit of conversation when she answered a fan who asked her "how long Pete is" — referring to a musical interlude on her album Sweetener — with "Like 10 inches? …oh f----…i mean…like a lil over a minute," she wrote jokingly at the time. (She also wrote "HUUUUGE" on his Burn Book page in the "Thank U, Next" video, continuing to feed the rumor mill.)

    The two have since broken up, and Davidson wants to make one thing clear: He doesn't want to have to live up to the hype, and frankly, doesn't agree with her assessment in the first place.

    "It's just simply not true," he said in his Netflix standup special, while addressing their fraught split. "She has tiny little hands. Everything is f------ huge to her ... Every girl that sees [it] for the rest of my life is [going to be] disappointed."

    Jason Derulo

    He captioned the new post: "F--- u mean? I have underwear on… I can't help my size.." and then (lest you were confused) clarified even further by commenting #bringbackanaconda.

    Michael Fassbender

    While the critically acclaimed actor has stayed mum on the subject, his Prometheus co-star, Charlize Theron, not-so-subtly shared her thoughts on Fassbender (and his nude scene in Shame) when accepting her Ally for Equality Award, presented to her by the actor, at the 2012 Human Rights Campaign Gala. Most other actors would have gone small, trust me. No, I know because I have worked with them."

    And she wasn't alone in praising Fassbender's, err, asset — George Clooney had similar things to say. "Really Michael, honestly, you can play golf like this with your hands behind your back," Clooney said, addressing Fassbender at the 2012 Golden Globes.

"Yeah… I had the good fortune to get to see him… all there," Clarkson said on Watch What Happens Live, claiming that Timberlake was "big."

    "They were shooting a scene that he couldn't keep anything on," she explained. "And he is a… gorgeous man."

    Adrian Grenier
