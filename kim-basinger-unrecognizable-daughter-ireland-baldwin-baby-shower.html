    HOLLYWOOD actress Kim Basinger looked unrecognizable as she arrived at her daughter's baby shower this week.

    Kim, 69, headed to Hollywood bar Jumbo's Clown Room where her daughter Ireland Baldwin was enjoying a strip club-themed celebration.

    The L.A. Confidential actress was keen to get in on the fun and wore a gray blazer with matching pants and a smart black shirt underneath.

    Kim completed her look with shiny black shoes and a camel-colored handbag, wearing her blond hair down straight.

    She was accompanied to the party by her boyfriend Mitch Stone, who stood out in an electric blue blazer that he paired with dark denim jeans and a black V-neck T-shirt.

    Ireland, 27, whose father is Alec Baldwin, went all out for her shower and wore a bright pink wig with a skimpy black lace dress.

    The mom-to-be teamed the racy garment with pink shiny cowboy boots and held hands with her boyfriend RAC as she made her way inside the venue.

    Once inside, it seems Ireland's baby shower was far from traditional, as she was surrounded by strippers, dollar bill notes, and greasy-looking cheeseburgers.

    At one point, some of the scantily-dressed dancers gave Ireland her very own lap dance and she appeared to be loving life.

    The women wore fake baby bumps and paraded around a pole as Ireland and her nearest and dearest cheered them on.

    In one of the photos, Kim is resting her head on Ireland's chest as the model laughs and has money stuffed down her top and on her stomach.

    Not all of Ireland's fans were overly impressed with the theme of the baby shower and shared their thoughts in the comment section.

    "How classy…said no one! SMH," one person said, while another claimed: "This is what’s wrong with the world today."

    A third wrote: "Because being "normal" is just not a thing anymore," and a fourth fumed: "This is absolutely disgusting in every way. Have more class!"

    "Tacky OMG," someone else simply wrote, while another added: "How wildly inappropriate."

    Others thought it was hilarious as one person wrote: "I love everything about this! You are so awesome!"

    Ireland was born in October 1995 during Kim's marriage to Hollywood actor Alec, which ended in divorce in 2002.

    The couple separated in 2000 and their split was far from amicable.

    In his 2008 book, A Promise to Ourselves: A Journey Through Fatherhood and Divorce, Alec chronicled the contentious seven-year custody battle with Kim over Ireland following their separation, contending that she spent more than $1.5 million in her efforts to deny him parental access.
