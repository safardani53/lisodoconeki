    ANNA Haugh has seen her star soar as a celebrity chef.

    The talented star is now riding high as one of the MasterChef: Professionals judges.

    Who is Anna Haugh?

    Anna Haugh was born on November 6, 1980 in Dublin, Ireland.

    She is best known for being an Irish chef, restaurateur and TV personality.

    Anna owns a restaurant called Myrtle located in Chelsea.

    The posh restaurant offers a range of modern European cuisines prepared with an Irish influence.

    Read More on MasterChef

    How did Anna Haugh's career start and how did she become famous?

    Head chef and restaurant owner Anna Haugh has been cooking professionally for almost 20 years.

    Anna first garnered national attention in 2004 when she appeared on BBC's The Stress Test.

    She also appeared on BBC One’s Royal Recipes between 2017 and 2018.

    She rose to fame for her cooking talents which she has showcased on a number of shows.

    Most read in Showbiz

    What TV shows has Anna Haugh appeared on?

    Anna makes regular appearances on shows such as BBC One’s Morning Live and Saturday Kitchen.

    She was also one of the main chefs on BBC’s Ready Steady Cook and has also appeared on Channel 4’s Snackmasters.

    Anna has also appeared on three episodes of Sunday Brunch and two episodes of Pointless Celebrities.

    In 2022 she was announced to be the new judge on MasterChef: Professionals, alongside judges Marcus Wareing and Gregg Wallace.

    Whilst visiting BBC Morning Live, Anna joked playfully with host Kym Marsh and said: “I’m going to be really tough, really mean, I’m going to start sabotaging."

    She later admitted she was joking and is looking forward judging new talent.

    Is Anna Haugh married and how many children does she have?

    Anna welcomed her first son Oisin back in August 2021.
