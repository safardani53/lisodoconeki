    FORMER celebrity stylist Tamaran claimed Jennifer Lopez, Jessica Alba and Katherine Heigl are some of the worst stars she worked with in a scathing Tik Tok.

    The stylist took it to the video-sharing platform and shared some of the famous faces that haven't been very pleasant to style or work with, including the Hustlers actress

    Tamaran dished on Jennifer: "I knew this one for years. There’s a reason why she can’t hold down a costumer or a seamstress.

    "In order to talk to JLo you can't look at her. In order to talk to her you have to talk to God."

    Tamaran also did not go easy on Jessica, who is the founder of the Honest Company.

    She claimed: "If 2020 was an attitude it would be Jessica Alba...she's not nice.

    “She loves to rub hummus on her dress, play mind games with you, [and] she does this thing where she talks to you while not talking to you.”

    The Grey's Anatomy alum also felt the brunt of things.

    She alleged: “If 2020 was a career, it would be Katherine Heigl. Friends of mine who have worked on set with her and photoshoots have told me that she is extremely difficult and always mad.”

    Tamaran, who worked for numerous celebrity stylists from 2008 to 2017, also chimed in on what it was like working with actress Marissa Tomei - and it wasn't a walk in the park as the Oscar winner showed up three hours late to her appointment.

    She claimed: “Marisa didn’t wake up until 12:30 p.m., barely says hello, and proceeds to eat her breakfast of yogurt and granola."

    Tamaran added that Marisa was also "extremely particular" and ended up returning a lot of items.

    People were loving Tamaran's celebrity revelations as they took to the comments section to respond.

    One person said: "I’ve always heard KH was an absolute monster. But I’m kinda shocked about JA & JLo."

    Another added: "The best part was as soon as you said that some were not a pleasure to work with [sic] Katharine Heigl was first in my mind."

    While some fans were quite surprised about Jennifer and Jessica's behavior, there seemed to be a clear consensus on Katherine, especially since she has not been spoken about in the most positive light in years.
