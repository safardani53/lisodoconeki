    Rihanna’s ‘Concert For Valor’ Performance Outfit — Sparkles In Tom Ford:

    Her look, from the designer’s Spring 2015 collection, sparkled from head-to-toe thanks to the full-on sequin embellishments. Her bodysuit also had a matching black cape that flowed around her, adding drama to the outfit. With a statement necklace and rings on practically every finger, she really elevated the outfit, adding a major glam factor that was also quite elegant to the look. Manolo Blahnik sandals rounded out the look.

    With her hair worn down, straight down her back in a dramatic side part and a bold red lip, (which matched her red mani!), everything about her performance outfit was flawless — and we loved it! Whether she’s all covered up or wearing next to nothing, one thing is for sure — RiRi’s style always has us doing a double take.

    She performed “Stay” and “Diamonds” and was joined by Eminem to perform their hit “Monster.”

    RiRi always dresses to impress, and her concert get-up was no exception. Do you agree? Are you as smitten by Rihanna’s style as we are?
