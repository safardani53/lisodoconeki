    Who's in the line-up for Wireless festival 2019?

    The gates open at 1.30pm TODAY (July 7) with the music begins at 3.30pm.

    What's the weather looking to be like during Wireless?

    Festival-goers are set to have three joyous rain-free days in store.

    There won't be a repeat of the 34C sunshine on the Saturday the week before, but music fans might welcome slightly less roasting conditions as they dance to their favourite acts.

    So pack your sun cream and tentatively leave your raincoats at home... or maybe bring them just in case.

    Friday, July 5

    Temperatures are set to reach highs of 26C on Friday - and will stay that warm as late as 7pm.

    The Met Office has forecast light winds a maximum temperature 28C.

    Saturday, July 6

    Saturday will be just slightly cooler, with current forecasts giving a high of 26C and lows of 15C.

    Despite the warmth it will be significantly cloudier.

    But the likelihood of rain is still low, with the Met Office predicting a 10 per cent chance.

    Sunday, July 7

    Sunday won't be quite as sunny, but temperatures will remain warm.

    The Met Office has forecast an average of 22C, with lows of 15CF.

    How can you get tickets to Wireless?

    Wireless tickets were able to purchase here.

    But unfortunately since it's just around the corner, all tickets are now sold out.

    You had the option of buying a single day pass or purchasing a two day or three day Weekend pass.

    Day tickets started at £65 (plus £8 booking fee) while weekend passes set you back up to £175 (plus £15 booking fee.)

    As soon as we know about details for 2020 we will update the info here.

    What happened to Cardi B last year?

    Cardi B will headline Wireless Festival 2019 one year after cancelling her appearance because she was pregnant.

    The American rapper will kick-start the event this summer while Travis Scott will headline Saturday and A$AP Rocky on Sunday.

    Cardi B is being given a second chance to play at Wireless Festival after missing out in 2018.

    Wireless Festival released a statement at the time saying: "Huge news over the weekend! Congratulations to @iamcardib! Unfortunately this means she won't be performing at Wireless this year."

    Cardi B gave birth to her daughter Kulture Kiari Cephus on July 10.

    More on festivals and concerts

    What do I need to know about Wireless?

    • Wireless is not a camping festival.
    • It is not suitable for young children. Children under 5 (aged 4 or below) are not permitted..
    • Children under 16 years old (aged 5 -15) must be accompanied by a parent or guardian (18 or over) at all times and will need a full-price ticket..
    • You can take snacks and soft drinks into the festival .
    • Large bags are not allowed to be taken into the festival. There is no cloakroom or storage facilities.
