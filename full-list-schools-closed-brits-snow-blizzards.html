    SCHOOLS across the UK are closed again today due to heavy snow and freezing temperatures.

    Thousands of pupils will miss class while Met Office yellow and amber weather warnings are in force.

    The Met Office has issued an amber warning for "strong winds bringing blizzard conditions" and up to 16 inches of snow to the area stretching from Stoke-on-Trent to Durham.

    A separate amber alert covers parts of Wales and advises of similar "significant disruption".

    Meanwhile, yellow warnings for snow cover vast stretches of the UK - from Bath and into Scotland.

    Those with children should check with individual schools or their local council website to confirm any closures.
