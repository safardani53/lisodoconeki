    02 Jul 2024, 00:00

    With the 10th season of ABC’s The Bachelorette coming down to an epic conclusion Monday night, Andi Dorfman has already made up her mind.

    She gave out her final rose in May, and The Bachelorette spoilers offer all you need to know about who we think got it, and what happens after that.

    Here we’re tackling a different, and less complex topic: Which of her final two dudes, Nick Viall and Josh Muraay, is hotter? Specifically, with no shirt?

    At first, superficial glance, former pro ballplayer Josh is the "hot" one of Andi Dorfman’s final two, with his sculpted physique and athletic pedigree.

    Nick is no slouch, though, as this throwback Instagram photo proves. The Milwaukee native is pretty chiseled in his own right, without being overstated.

    Maybe the underdog Nick can actually win this poll for that reason? Or does Josh’s muscle-bound glory trump all just like you’d expect it would?

    Only your votes can decide.

     

    As for who Andi picks and why, we’ll have to wait a few more days to find out. What we’re most curious about going into the finale is what’s in the note.

    Either way, there’s going to be some heartbreak, as both guys are in this to win it, but on the bright side, one of the two will be back on the market soon.

    The same can’t be said for the engaged Marcus Grodd, or his fellow hometown date recipient Chris Soules, who is likely to be the next Bachelor.
