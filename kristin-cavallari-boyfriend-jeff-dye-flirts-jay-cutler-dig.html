    KRISTIN Cavallari's new "boyfriend" Jeff Dye made his presence known on the star's Instagram with a flirty comment following ex-husband Jay Cutler's public dig.

    The former couple tied the knot in 2013 before parting ways in April 2020.

    The comedian took to Kristin's Instagram recently after the reality star shared a photo of herself modeling jewelry for her Uncommon James line.

    In the snap, the 33-year-old exposes her neck to show off a selection of silver jewelry.

    And that was enough to prompt a response from the 37-year-old actor who commented with a heart-eye emoji.

    Kristin and Jeff have been spotted together a few times, with one of the first outings dating back to October.

    At the time, TMZ obtained video of the pair locking lips while in Chicago.

    Jeff's emoji comment comes after Kristin's ex-husband threw some shade at the star over the Thanksgiving weekend.

    The former NFL star, 37, took to Instagram to share a video of him clinking wine glasses with Shannon Ford - who fans of the show Very Cavallari will remember was a fixture in the early days of the program until Kristin fired her.

    According to Kristin, Shannon had been missing meetings, not showing up to the office, ignoring deadlines, and being outright rude to the boss herself.

    After the sacking, Shannon posted a series of shade-worth Instagram photos leading many to speculate that the two’s once firm friendship was well and truly over.

    Previously, Kristin also threw some shade at her former partner, as she shared a quote that read: “Happiness is an inside job. Don’t assign anyone else that much power over your life.”

    The Laguna Beach alum announced her split from Jay earlier this year, as she would admit that it was the "hardest decision" she's ever made.

    She previously told People: "I always thought it was interesting when people would refer to us as 'couple goals.'"

    Kristin added: "We tried really, really hard for years and years. It was the hardest decision I've ever made."

    The former pair share three kids: sons Camden, eight, and Jaxon, six, and 5-year-old daughter Saylor.
