    Easter 2023: How Kardashian-Jenner Family Celebrated in Photos

    06 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A hoppy family affair! True to fashion, the Kardashian-Jenner brood went all out to celebrate Easter on Sunday, April 9.

    “Happy easter,” Kylie Jenner captioned a Sunday Instagram snap of herself holding a basket of plastic eggs to set up for the family’s annual hunt.

    The Kylie Cosmetics mogul, 25, stunned in her vintage, green Jean-Paul Gaultier gown as she showed off the party decor. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum uploaded a series of Instagram Story pics of a garden-inspired tablescape and special treats for the kids.

    Stormi and Aire, Jenner’s two children with ex-boyfriend Travis Scott, each received giant personalized gift baskets. An array of toys, including L.O.L. Surprise! dolls and a Push-Along Toy Rabbit, can be seen inside the arrangement.

    Jenner’s big sister Khloé Kardashian also shared glimpses of the festive bash via her Stories, including footage of daughter True, 4, decorating eggs with an Egg-Mazing kit. True — whom the 38-year-old Good American founder shares with ex Tristan Thompson, alongside a baby boy — looked pretty in pink in her dress and matching bunny ears. True, who turns 5 later this month, completed her look with a personalized necklace that featured her name encrusted in diamonds.

    The siblings’ mother, Kris Jenner, had shared a peek into her preparation for the special celebration one day earlier. The Kardashians matriarch, 67, cooed over baked goods from Crumbles Patisserie, which included cookies decorated with each of her 12 grandchildren’s names. (Kris is a doting “Lovey” to her children Kim Kardashian, Kourtney Kardashian, Rob Kardashian, Khloé and Kylie’s respective little ones.) Kris also received custom Lily and Bean handbags for granddaughters True, Stormi, Chicago, North and Penelope.

    “Wishing everyone a happy Easter weekend. #Easter #Family #Memories #AlwaysMatching,” the Safely founder captioned a Friday, April 7, Instagram post as she shared a variety of throwback Easter pics of her daughters wearing coordinating outfits.

    Kourtney, 43, jokingly replied: “Recreate the red dresses?”
