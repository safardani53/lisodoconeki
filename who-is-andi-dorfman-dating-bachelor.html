    BACHELORETTE star Andi Dorfman is still looking for her Prince Charming.

    Nine months after getting engaged to Josh Murry in the season 10 finale of The Bachelorette, the relationship has reportedly reached its end.

    Who is Andi Dorfman?

    Andi Dorfman, 33, was born in Atlanta, the youngest of two siblings.

    The 33-year-old is an American television personality star and a former assistant District Attorney for Fulton County, Georgia.

    She rose to stardom after being a contestant on season 18 of The Bachelor, and the star of season 10 of The Bachelorette.

    Her spotlight moment came during The Bachelor, when she famously walked out on bachelor Juan Pablo Galavis in the ninth episode of the season.

    Dorfman became the first Bachelorette to acknowledge being of Jewish ancestry in the show's history, and the first attorney to appear in the role.

    What shows has Andi Dorfman appeared in?

    Dorfman appeared in season 18 of The Bachelor during Pablo Galavis' season.

    She then starred in season 10 of The Bachelorette, which premiered in 2014.

    Dorfman took a leave from her job as assistant district attorney to be on the show full-time.

    Who is Josh Murray?

    During the season finale of The Bachelorette, Dorfman selected Murray as the winner.

    On May 9, 2014, Dorfman and Murray announced their engagement.

    However, the couple split on January 8, 2015, just nine months after announcing their engagement.

    Is Andi Dorfman dating?

    It's been over five years since the end of her Bachelorette season, and it appears Dorfman still isn't married or dating.

    Dorfman was recently spotted with Nick Viall, another former contestant in The Bachelor.

    Nevertheless, a source close to Dorfman quickly shot down the rumors that the couple were in a relationship.

    "Andi and Nick are friends," a source close to Dorfman told PEOPLE.

    "She just moved to L.A. so they met up for a run."
