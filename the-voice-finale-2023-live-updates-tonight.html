    Meet next seasons coach: Dan + Shay

    Dan & Shay will join Reba McEntire, John Legend, and Chance the Rapper for Season 25, which will air in the spring of 2024.

    The country duo will sit at the previously-teased double chair, according to TV Line.

    Variety reported the news just months before Season 24 aired this fall, which featured Niall Horan, Gwen Stefani, Reba, and John as coaches.

    Niall is most likely not returning in 2024, due to his world tour.

    A truly star-studded event

    On top of the contestants and coaches performing on last night's finale, the conclusion of season 24 saw performances from:

    Fans slam Gwen Stefani's outfit

    Gwen, 54, showed off one of her bold ensembles for part one of The Voice finale Monday.

    The television star posed in her dressing room, pouting as she directed her sultry gaze away from the camera.

    Gwen had her blond hair tied back, with a messy bun high on her head while two curled strands fell in front of her face.

    She wore a full face of flawless makeup, with false lashes reaching almost up to her brows, glittery eyeshadow, and a pink lipstick.

    One person exclaimed: "This look for the win!! It’s beyond!!!"

    A second agreed: "A very beautiful dress! Gwen is amazing as always," along with fire and heart emojis.

    Meet last year's winner Gina Miles

    After weeks of competition, Season 23 of The Voice came down to five artists from Team Niall, Team Blake, Team Chance, and Team Kelly.

    In the end, the last performer standing on stage was Gina Miles.

    Along with taking home the title, Miles also received $100,000 and a record deal with Universal Music Group.

    What to know about your Season 24 coaches – John Legend

    Coach John Legend took his last Team Legend artist, Lila Forde, to the Season 24 finale of The Voice.

    John is a highly acclaimed, multi-platinum-selling singer-songwriter who has won 10 Grammy Awards, an Academy Award, a Golden Globe, a Tony Award, and an Emmy Award, among other honors. 

    He is the first Black man to receive an EGOT and one of only 15 members of the exclusive EGOT club. 

    John has been married to model Chrissy Teigen since 2013, and the pair have welcomed four children.

    What to know about your Season 24 coaches – Gwen Stefani

    Gwen Stefani is in her seventh season as a coach on The Voice, and sadly did not have a chance at winning Season 24 with no Team Gwen artists in the finale.

    Stefani has gained global success as a composer, vocalist, and charismatic front lady of No Doubt, as well as a multi-platinum solo artist. 

    She is a three-time Grammy Award winner. Throughout it all, she has remained a music and fashion star, winning four MTV Video Music Awards, two Billboard Awards, an American Music Award, and a Brit Award.

    She has sold over 60 million sales worldwide, including her four-time platinum debut solo album Love. Angel. Music. Baby. which included the smash songs Rich Girl, What You Waiting For? and Hollaback Girl.

    Gwen is married to Blake Shelton, whom she met when they were both coaches on The Voice in 2014. The duo started dating in 2015 and tied the knot in 2021.
