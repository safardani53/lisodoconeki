    A BENTLEY Mulsanne once used by King Charles' protection officers is up for sale for £75k.

    The Monarch himself drove in the swish motor - which has an amazing selection of hidden features.

    The Bentley was a special operations model made for the Royal Household.

    The interiors are finished in a dark sapphire colour, with leather upholstery.

    It also has some rather unusual bonus features.

    It has built-in picnic tables perfect for lunch on the go.

    Cruise control, keyless start, soft close doors and rain sensing wipers are also a standard on the royal vehicle.
