    “My guardian angel. I miss you so damn much mom. I love being told I look like you. I love you,” Dalis tweeted on Dec. 10 along with a side-by-side pic of her mom and herself. Although Dalis didn’t elaborate the cause of her mom’s death, she later tweeted that she and her older brother were now hanging out in heaven, and she loved thinking about that.

    But amid such personal tragedy, Dalis apparently decided to cut ties with another important figure in her life — her boyfriend Ryan. “I feel so damn free! #finally,” she tweeted on Dec. 13.

    Ryan echoed the breakup news on Dec. 16, when a follower on Twitter asked if he and Dalis were still together, and he replied “no.”

    On Dec. 17, Dalis revealed one of her favorite quotes on her personal blog dalisconnell.blogspot.com: “Exit the people in your life that try to bring you down. Spend more time with the people who build you up because then you, can truly succeed.” Sounds like she’s talking about Ryan!

    Dalis’ Wild Night Out With Kyle King

    But while fans are buzzing with the hopeful news that Maci and Ryan might rekindle their old flame — she’s been single since she split with longtime boyfriend Kyle King in September — it looks like Kyle and Dalis might be the ones cozying up together!

    On Dec. 16, Dalis shared a pic on Instagram of her out with Kyle and a couple other friends, and wrote “Wonder how much s**t we will get for this! hahaha don’t currr @kyleking100.”

    Even though the two are standing very close together in the pic, we wonder if it had more to do with making their respective exes jealous — and less about them actually dating.

    Either way, we think both Dalis and Maci are too good for Ryan! But Dalis and Ryan have split up and gotten back together before, so we’ll have to wait and see what happens.
